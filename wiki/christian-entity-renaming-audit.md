# Christian Entity Renaming Audit

## Purpose

This audit identifies Before We Build research entities that should be kept, renamed, demoted, or hidden when writing for a Christian public audience, especially Scripture-first / Baptist / Sola Scriptura-sensitive readers.

It complements [[glossary-audience-translation]]:

- the glossary defines audience translations;
- this audit makes editorial decisions about concrete entity families in the current research wiki.

## Scan basis

A repository scan of `wiki/**/*.md` found the highest concentration of potentially unsafe Christian-public terms in:

- `wiki/glossary-core.md`
- `wiki/glossary-extended.md`
- `wiki/concepts/plan.md`
- `wiki/concepts/compatibility-level-boundaries.md`
- `wiki/concepts/main-idea.md`
- `wiki/concepts/project-positioning.md`
- `wiki/concepts/latent-process.md`
- `wiki/concepts/typology-test-design-protocol.md`
- `wiki/relations/*-intertype-relations.md`
- `wiki/entities/*` type pages
- `wiki/sources/*` research/source pages

These pages can keep technical language as research pages, but Christian-facing pages should not use that language as the first doorway.

## Decision categories

- **KEEP** — safe or useful in Christian public language.
- **RENAME** — concept may remain, but public wording should change.
- **DEMOTE** — allowed only below the first doorway, with caveats.
- **HIDE** — keep in research layer only; do not use in ordinary Christian public copy.
- **PASTOR CHECK** — wording may be acceptable but needs human/pastoral review because it touches doctrine, spiritual authority, church care, or Scripture interpretation.

## Typology system decisions

### Socionics

**Research role:** tactical-level information modeling / communication-process schema.

**Christian-public decision:** **HIDE in first doorway; DEMOTE in deep explanation.**

**Christian-facing replacement:**

- how we speak and listen;
- how we understand or miss each other;
- repeated patterns in communication;
- questions about conversation and repair.

**General-audience replacement:**

- information-processing schema;
- communication pattern map.

**Notes:** Do not show Model A, Te/Ti/Fe/Fi, quadras, information metabolism, or intertype relations in ordinary Christian copy. If disclosed in research, mark it as a heuristic source tradition, not validated science and not spiritual insight.

### Psychosophy / Attitudinal Psyche

**Research role:** operational-level action, decision, reasoning, emotion, will, and practical domain schema.

**Christian-public decision:** **HIDE in first doorway; RENAME concepts into human questions.**

**Christian-facing replacement:**

- how we choose and carry responsibility;
- how we reason and explain;
- how we receive and express feelings;
- how we care for body, home, money, and practical needs.

**General-audience replacement:**

- decision-and-action map;
- attitudes-to-domains schema.

**Notes:** Avoid public words such as “Psychosophy,” “Physics,” “function,” and “position.” “Will” needs special care because Christian readers may connect it to obedience, pride, submission, or spiritual maturity.

### Temporistics

**Research role:** strategic-level time, meaning, memory, hope, fear, and existential orientation schema.

**Christian-public decision:** **HIDE system name; RENAME aspects.**

**Christian-facing replacements:**

- Past → memory, story, wounds, gratitude, what needs forgiveness or learning;
- Present → belonging, place, current obedience, attention, contact;
- Future → direction, promises, planning, hope, fear;
- Eternity → meaning, calling, ultimate questions before God.

**General-audience replacements:**

- past orientation;
- present orientation;
- future orientation;
- meaning orientation.

**Special warning:** “Eternity” should usually be renamed in Christian public pages because it can sound like a doctrinal claim rather than a research category.

## Application entity decisions

### Relationships and marriage

**Keep central Christian terms:** foundation, shared faith, character, maturity, counsel, prayer, family, responsibility, forgiveness, wise next step.

**Rename:**

- compatibility → questions before a serious step;
- matching → possible introduction / possible conversation;
- profile → notes for conversation;
- result → themes to discuss.

**Avoid:** ideal match, chosen spouse, guaranteed family, score, automated match, spiritual verdict.

**Pastor check:** any claim about marriage, divorce, unequal yoking, church authority, or “God’s will for this relationship.”

### Family

**Keep:** forgiveness, patience, repair, trust, household responsibility, communication, wise counsel.

**Rename internal terms:**

- conflict pattern → repeated family tension;
- interaction relation → where we help or wound each other;
- profile → family conversation notes.

**Avoid:** diagnosing family members by type.

### Church service

**Keep:** one body, many members; gifts; weaknesses; mutual need; service; humility; care.

**Rename internal terms:**

- role fit → where a person can serve faithfully;
- team design → serving together with clear responsibility;
- type/function → gifts, limits, and responsibilities to discuss.

**Pastor check:** any language that could sound like assigning spiritual rank or ministry worth by typology.

### Team / common work

**Keep:** responsibility, clear agreements, trust, decisions, workload, conflict repair.

**Christian-facing replacement:** building common work faithfully.

**General-facing replacement:** team fit / collaboration map.

**Avoid:** treating people as resources or optimization units.

### Business partnership

**Keep:** honesty, word, money, risk, power, exit rules, responsibility before God.

**Rename:** compatibility → partnership questions before starting.

**Pastor/human check:** claims about debt, financial ethics, authority, and vows/promises.

### Role and responsibility

**Keep:** faithful service, limits, support needs, not pretending to be another member.

**Rename:** role fit → where a person can carry responsibility faithfully.

**Avoid:** fit/unfit, useful/useless, high-value/low-value person.

## Page-level recommendations

### `wiki/glossary-core.md`

**Current role:** authoritative technical glossary.

**Decision:** Keep as research glossary, but add/keep a visible pointer to [[glossary-audience-translation]] for public language.

**Risk:** It currently contains many technical terms: Persona, Digital Twin, simulation, compatibility, model, scoring. These should not leak into public Christian copy.

### `wiki/glossary-extended.md`

**Current role:** disambiguation of ambiguous research/technical terms.

**Decision:** Keep as research reference. Do not use as public copy source.

**Risk:** It reinforces technical precision, which is useful for agents but not for ordinary Christian visitors.

### `wiki/concepts/main-idea.md`

**Decision:** Needs a Christian-audience alternate summary or pointer.

**Recommended public summary:**

> Before We Build helps people pause before serious shared decisions, see the foundation, and name questions for honest conversation. For Christians, the first foundation is not a type or score but Scripture, character, wisdom, prayer, and counsel.

### `wiki/concepts/project-positioning.md`

**Decision:** Keep research/product positioning, but separate Christian public doorway from Cognitive Matchmaker and AI matching language.

**Risk:** “AI dating concierge,” “simulation-based compatibility assessment,” and similar phrases are bad first-doorway language for Christians.

### `wiki/concepts/compatibility-level-boundaries.md`

**Decision:** Useful internally, but Christian pages should translate levels into questions.

**Rename levels publicly:**

- Strategic → Where are we going before God?
- Operational → How do we carry responsibility and make decisions?
- Tactical → How do we speak, listen, and repair?

### `wiki/concepts/latent-process.md`

**Decision:** Keep in research layer; translate publicly as repeated patterns or hidden habits.

**Warning:** Do not imply the system can see the heart. Only God sees the heart fully.

### `wiki/concepts/test-result-reading-guide.md`

**Decision:** Rename public framing away from “test result” where possible.

**Christian-facing title option:**

> How to read the conversation map

or:

> How to use your answers without turning them into a verdict

### `wiki/entities/*` type pages

**Decision:** Keep as research/entity pages. Do not route ordinary Christian visitors directly to raw type pages.

**If linked from public site:** introduce through “possible themes for conversation,” not “this is who you are.”

### `wiki/relations/*-intertype-relations.md`

**Decision:** Keep as research. Public pages should translate relation names into interaction questions.

**Example replacement:**

- “conflict relation” → “where we may repeatedly misunderstand or pressure each other”;
- “dual/complement” → “where help may be possible, but character and responsibility still matter.”

## Required implementation checks for future edits

When updating public copy or site pages, run these checks:

1. Search public HTML/JS for:
   - `compatibility`, `совместим`, `сумісн`
   - `profile`, `профил`, `профіль`
   - `type`, `тип`, `типолог`
   - `score`, `балл`, `процент`, `відсот`
   - `simulation`, `prediction`, `digital twin`, `model`, `engine`
   - `research`, `methodology`, `validation`, `wiki`
2. Check whether each occurrence is:
   - ordinary public doorway;
   - deep research page;
   - code identifier only;
   - visible label.
3. Replace visible public labels first; do not blindly rename code identifiers.
4. Run theological/plain-language review after replacements.
5. For Christian normative claims, add nearby Scripture grounding according to [[biblical-grounding-policy]].

3. How should “wise counsel” be described so it encourages church care without enabling control or curiosity?

5. Which terms should be banned entirely from Christian-facing headings: compatibility, test, profile, type, model, score?

## Final audit recommendation

For Christian-facing Before We Build copy, the default translation should be:

```text
internal research entity → human question → Scripture-near framing → conversation map → wise next step
```

Do not expose the raw entity unless the visitor intentionally opens the research layer.

The highest-priority public replacements are:

1. **Compatibility → questions before a serious step / map for conversation**
2. **Test → simple questions / questions for discernment**
3. **Profile → notes for conversation / map of differences**
4. **Type → repeated pattern to discuss**
5. **Score → hidden or limited signal**
6. **Model/engine/simulation/digital twin → research layer only**
7. **Eternity → meaning / calling / ultimate questions before God**
